How to Play Checkers

Basic Movement

  • Click a piece to select it (it will highlight in blue)
  • Click a highlighted square to move there
  • Regular pieces move diagonally forward one square
  • Reach the opposite end to become a King â™”

Kings

  • Kings can move diagonally in any direction (forward or backward)
  • Kings are marked with a crown symbol â™”

Capturing

  • Jump over an opponent's piece to capture it
  • The captured piece is removed from the board
  • You can capture multiple pieces in one turn (multi-jump)

Important Rules

  • Mandatory Jumps: If a jump is available, you MUST take it
  • Multi-Jump: If you can jump again after a capture, you must continue
  • Win Condition: Capture all opponent pieces or block their moves
  • Draw: Game ends in a draw after 40 moves without a capture
